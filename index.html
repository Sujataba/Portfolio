About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I am a dedicated Database Professional with a rich and diverse background in the world of database management and Cloud Analytical services. As my career has evolved, so has my passion for data exploration and the quest for meaningful insights that drive critical business decisions.

With every passing day, I find myself more intrigued by the potential of data analysis and visualization. This growing enthusiasm has led me to pivot my career towards the captivating realms of Data Analysis and Data Visualization. My journey has been enriched through the consistent exploration of real-world data, allowing me to gain a profound understanding of how data fuels decision-making processes within organizations.

Over time, I have sharpened my analytical thinking and problem-solving skills while working on various tools, including Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Tableau, through a multitude of analytical projects. My aspiration now is to take this knowledge and transition into Data Science, with a primary goal of aiding businesses in making more informed and data-driven decisions.

Beyond my professional life, I'm an avid enthusiast who discovers joy in crafting with recycled materials during my spare moments. My vision is to infuse this creativity into the world of data by presenting it in imaginative and compelling ways that resonate with customers.
