NSW Road Crash Dashboard

The objective of this project is to analyze NSW Road crash data over a period of five years (2017-2021) in order to gain insights into the following requirements::
Requirement:
* Generate KPIs - Total casualties & percentage of total with respect to accident severity and maximum casualties by type of vehicle.
* Road crash trend analysis on yearly, monthly , daywise and time interval wise.
* Casualties by varying conditions of Road , weather and light.
* LGAs having the highest number of crashes.
* Factors effecting crashes like location type and street type.

The Steps included:
1. Understanding the requirement and the data.
2. Identifying key insights.
3. Data cleaning as per the requirement.
4. Data Processing and preparationby adding some customized columns in data.
5. Data Analysis by Pivot Tables and Excel Functions to generate KPIs/Trend Analysis.
6. Data Visualization to create charts and custom sheets to show the insights.
7. Report/ Dashboard creation from start to end.
