    HR Analytics-Employee Attrition

    This Power BI case study investigates a dataset for a make-believe software firm named Atlas Labs. A thorough examination of attrition and its determinants has been carried out through data analysis
    Steps Used:
    1. Loading all data into Power BI and connecting the data model.
    2. Data cleaning and Transformations.
    3. Initial exploratory data analysis on general HR trends.
    4. Analyzing Demographics and Performance
    5. DAX Measures and Columns
    6. Conditional formatting
    7. Extracting insights using DAX.
    8. Building custom visuals and creating cohesive reports.
    9. Theme formatting and Page navigation
    9. Delivering insights on attrition and what factors affect employee retention.
